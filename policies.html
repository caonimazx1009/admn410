Our Policies

Shipping Charges & Times

Shipping costs are determined by the total weight of the order and by the shipping method selected.

Estimated shipping charges will be displayed in your Shopping Cart after you have signed in to your registered online account. Final shipping charges will be displayed during Checkout, once a shipping address has been entered and a shipping method has been selected. For all other customers, the Shipping cost will be displayed in Checkout once the Shipping Address is entered and the Shipping Method selected. Shipping to Alaska, Hawaii, Puerto Rico, the Virgin Islands or P.O. Boxes will result in a surcharge. This is based on your default shipping address.

We will ship our products during the weekday. Order number and Tracking will send to your email as soon as possible when you place a order. If you need a signature requirement, please leave a note.

Use your USPS shipment tracking number to confirm the delivery date of your package by visiting the USPS web site. Please Note that USPS does not track the progress of your shipment, just delivery confirmation. Sorry, we do not ship to addresses within the U.S. Virgin Islands.






Returns

We accept free return if you don't happy with your products. Our return policy is easy and fast. Please download Durham House of Pets(DHP) App and click return items. Then you will get a return label if you want to return items by mail. If you want to return items in store, please bring items to the nearest store and drop items in the reception desk. Please make sure items are not broken.






Financing

We accept VISA, AmericanExpress, PayPal and GiftCard for checking out. We will not charge your money until items are shipping. Please make sure your billing address is correct. We have strict policy for the billing address.


Price Matching

We're committed to providing the highest quality products at the best prices. If you find a lower price for an identical item at any competitor's store, we'll gladly match it.


Pricing Errors and Omissions

We make every effort to ensure the accuracy of the information on our website. If an error is discovered it si immediately corrected. Please be advised that Durham House of Pets reserves the right to revoke any stated offer and to correct any errors, inaccuracies or omissions, including after an order has been submitted, after it has been confirmed, or after your credit card has been charged. If we discover a pricing error after your credit card has bene charged and your order is canceled as result of the error, your credit card will be refunded back the full amount of your order.
